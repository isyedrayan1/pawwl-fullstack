

# Pawwl — Pet Care Website

## Design System
- **Primary color**: Navy/dark blue (#1B3A5C), Teal/cyan accent (#2EC4B6)
- **Background**: White (#FFFFFF), Light sections with soft gray
- **Footer**: Dark navy (#0F2A44)
- **Fonts**: Rounded playful serif for headings (Playfair Display or similar), clean sans-serif for body (Inter/Poppins)
- **Border radius**: Rounded (16-24px for cards), pill-shaped buttons
- **Style**: Friendly, warm, pet-focused with playful typography

## Sections (top to bottom)

### 1. Navbar
- Logo "Pawwl" on left, nav links (Home, About Us, Our Products, Pet News, Contact), search & cart icons, "Pet Profile" CTA button (teal)

### 2. Hero Banner
- Large teal background with "Pawwl" in big playful white text, hero image of dog with accessories

### 3. Featured Products
- Left: Product card with image (Super Chewer treats), Right: Two stacked product descriptions with "Learn More" buttons
- Below: Row of 3 small product cards (dog food items)
- Below that: Row of 4 category cards with icons (Accessories, Dog Care, etc.)

### 4. Why Pawwl? Section
- Heading + subtitle text
- Left: Large "50K+" stat with label, Right: 4 feature cards in 2x2 grid (Certified Experts, Flexible Hours, Community 100%, Breeds Care)

### 5. Blogs Section
- Heading + subtitle
- Two blog post cards with images, titles "Premium Grooming Care", and "Read More" buttons

### 6. Born from Love Section
- Image of dogs on left, text + subtitle on right
- Stats row: 8+ years, 50+ products, 10K+ customers

### 7. Schedule a Session
- Left: Overlapping images collage with promotional badges
- Right: "Pawwl Pet Services" form/info card with service details and booking info

### 8. Pet Gallery Section
- "Whiskers, Tails, and Joyful Eyes" heading
- Grid of pet photos with "Join our pack" overlay button

### 9. Testimonials
- "What other Pawrents say" heading
- Testimonial cards with quotes, star ratings, user avatars

### 10. CTA Section
- Dark navy background, "Ready to Give Your Pet the Best Life?" heading
- Two buttons: "Explore Products" and "Book a Session"

### 11. Newsletter Signup
- Email input with subscribe button

### 12. Footer
- Logo, navigation columns, social links, copyright

## Implementation
- Single-page layout with all sections as separate React components
- Placeholder images using Unsplash pet photos via URLs
- Fully responsive (mobile + desktop)
- Smooth scroll, hover animations on cards and buttons
- All built with Tailwind CSS + shadcn/ui components
